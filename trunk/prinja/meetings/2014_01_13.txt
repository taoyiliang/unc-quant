

 - Analytic second moments
let f(X) = a+bx
 <f(x)^2>=<(a+bx)^2> = <a^2 + 2abx + b^2x^2>


 - SCPCE second moment
<f(x)^2> = < (sum x_n B_n)^2 >
         = < sum_n x_n B_n sum_m x_m B_m >
         = < sum_m sum_n x_m x_n B_n B_m >
         = sum_n (x_n)^2
